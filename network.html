ListingApe.com is a directory listing and management software that allows you to list and claim your business in our growing ListingApe Network of over 50 internet directories through one single entry. Set up is easy! Simply enter your business information at home or at your office and you’ll be able to control your business profile across the web. As an added bonus, we are continually adding new directories to our ListingApe Network. ListingApe.com also allows you to make changes to your business profile from a single login location. Because ListingApe.com claims your listings for you, it allows you to take control over how the search engines index you and prevents others from claiming your listings and controlling them.
